THE GRAND RESIDENCY

AGH Builders and Developers latest development, The Grand Residency is an exclusive residential project located in the heart of Bahria Town, Karachi.

The building comprises of Commercial property in the Basement and Ground Floor, while the First to Eighth floors are dedicated to 24 luxury residential apartments. The highlight of the project are three Duplex Penthouse apartments, spread across the Ninth and Tenth floors, that provide the best of ultraluxury living in the sky.

Designed by renowned architect Mr. Fahim Khatri, The Grand Residency brings all the luxuries and amenities of modern bungalow living to the apartment lifestyle. All apartments boast lush views of the surrounding vistas, spacious open floor planning, class leading materials and finishes, and an attention to detail that allows for a com- fortable and luxurious lifestyle.

The project is further complemented by such state-of-the-art facilities such as biometric security and key card access to the building, 24-hour CCTV surveillance, and dedicated firefighting and backup power services, thus providing a unique blend of comfort and security that lives up to the motto of 'Comfortable living for a better future.'
